📊 Typical Values Guide
Normal Range
  • Glucose: < 100 mg/dL (fasting)
  • Blood Pressure: < 120/80 mm Hg
  • BMI: 18.5 - 24.9
  • Skin Thickness: 10-30 mm
At Risk/Diabetic Range
  • Glucose: ≥ 126 mg/dL (fasting)
  • Blood Pressure: ≥ 140/90 mm Hg
  • BMI: ≥ 30 (Obese)
  • Age: ≥ 45 years
💡 Health Tips:
  • Maintain healthy weight and BMI
  • Exercise regularly (150 mins/week)
  • Eat balanced diet with whole grains
  • Monitor blood sugar if at risk
  • Get regular health check-ups
📚 Parameter Explanations

Plasma glucose concentration after 2 hours in an oral glucose tolerance test. Normal fasting glucose is less than 100 mg/dL.

A function that scores the likelihood of diabetes based on family history. Higher values indicate stronger family history of diabetes.
